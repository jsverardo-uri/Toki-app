# User Research Observations – Toki

## Observation 1

**Observation Statement:**  
Most users struggle to balance coursework with other responsibilities, especially work and personal commitments.

**Supporting Data:**  
- 6 out of 12 respondents selected “Balancing school with work/family” as their biggest challenge  
- 7 out of 12 respondents identified as “Full-time student with a job”

**Team Action (if any):**  
Prioritize features that reduce planning effort, such as automatic syllabus parsing and quick daily task views.

---

## Observation 2

**Observation Statement:**  
Users strongly value deadline awareness and reminders over other features.

**Supporting Data:**  
- 6 responses selected “Deadline reminders” as the most valuable feature (highest among all options)  
- Assignment dashboard was second with 4 responses

**Team Action (if any):**  
Ensure deadline visibility and reminder functionality are core features of the app’s design.

---

## Observation 3

**Observation Statement:**  
There is very high interest in AI-driven syllabus parsing functionality.

**Supporting Data:**  
- 10 out of 12 respondents rated the feature a 5 (extremely useful)  
- Remaining 2 rated it a 4

**Team Action (if any):**  
Make AI syllabus parsing a central feature of the app and highlight it in the UI and product messaging.

---

## Observation 4

**Observation Statement:**  
Users want additional functionality beyond basic tracking, including grade calculation and collaboration features.

**Supporting Data:**  
- Open-ended responses mentioned:
  - “Calculating my grades”
  - “Sharing or collaboration features”
  - “Reminders with enough time before deadlines”

**Team Action (if any):**  
Incorporate grade tracking and consider future expansion into collaboration features.
